# First-Month KPI Tracker

Track these numbers weekly.

- Blog posts published
- Landing page visits
- Email signups
- Booking page clicks
- Discovery calls booked
- Beta sessions completed
- Testimonials collected
- Paid sessions sold

## Validation targets

- At least 2 posts published by the end of week 2
- At least 5 discovery conversations or beta sessions in month 1
- At least 1 paid session within 30 to 60 days
- At least 1 testimonial before raising prices
