# Pricing and Packages

## Core pricing

- Resume + LinkedIn Review: $79
- Mock QA/SDET Interview: $99
- Portfolio Review: $89
- Interview Sprint Package: $239

## Beta offer guidance

- Offer the first 3 beta sessions at 20% off in exchange for a testimonial.
- Keep the beta limit visible so urgency feels real and controlled.
- Raise prices only after testimonials and repeatable positioning are in place.

## Positioning notes

- Lead with the resume review because it is the easiest entry point for buyers.
- Use the mock interview as the natural upsell for candidates already getting interviews.
- Use the portfolio review for candidates with GitHub projects who need stronger presentation.
